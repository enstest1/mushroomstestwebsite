Supplies

To grow magic mushrooms, you will need the following supplies:

  • Mushroom spores
  • Grow medium (such as straw or manure)
  • Containers (such as jars or bags)
  • Heat source (such as a heating pad)
  • Thermometer

You can purchase these supplies from specialized retailers or online stores.

Instructions

Follow these steps to grow magic mushrooms successfully:

  1. Inoculate the grow medium with the mushroom spores using a sterilized needle or spoon.
  2. Place the grow medium in a container and seal it tightly.
  3. Keep the container in a warm, dark place with a temperature of around 70-80°F (21-27°C).
  4. Monitor the temperature using a thermometer and adjust the heat source as needed.
  5. After a few weeks, you should see the mushroom mycelium starting to grow.
  6. When the mycelium has fully colonized the grow medium, expose the container to light to initiate the fruiting process.
  7. Harvest the mushrooms when they are fully mature.
